About

Broadcaster first, scientist second.

Seventeen years in broadcasting in India — television, education programming, the odd bit of published fiction — came before the PhD in computational biology. That order matters: the science came second, the storytelling never left.

These days that shows up as courses at Northeastern, a longevity book co-authored for Penguin Random House, a Substack, and a YouTube channel — all trying to do the same thing: make dense, technical ideas land the way a good story does.

Next chapter: the same toolkit, pointed at financial data and fintech.
